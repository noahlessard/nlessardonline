## March, 2026

This is a bit of a combined update for February and March, since I was pretty busy for February getting back into the swing of school work as well as club activities.

I also have been spending a lot of time in the <Link href="https://upl.cs.wisc.edu/">UPL</Link> this semester, which has been fun. They had a leftover raspberry Pi and TV, so I'm working on an ambient, 24/7, train multimedia experience.
The text you see being drawn on screen is actually being done with a transparent <Link href="https://codeberg.org/dnkl/foot">Foot</Link> terminal profile and <Link href="https://notcurses.com/">notcurses</Link>, which is giving me a chance to practice my C++ and Linux skills.
Future blog post coming soon about this project, probably.
